# Skill: Behavioral Analytics

Domain knowledge for responsible gaming pattern detection.

## Gamalyze Neuro-Markers (0-100)
- **Sensitivity to Reward**: >70 = "hot hand" fallacy
- **Sensitivity to Loss**: >75 = loss-chasing (STRONGEST PREDICTOR)
- **Risk Tolerance**: >80 = parlay/longshot bias
- **Decision Consistency**: <30 = erratic/impulsive

## Market Drift Dimensions

**Horizontal** (Sport Diversity):
- Normal: 1.2x baseline
- Risky: 3x+ baseline

**Vertical** (Market Quality):
- Tier 1.0: NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL
- Tier 0.5: MMA, Boxing, Tennis
- Tier 0.2: Table Tennis, Esports
- Risky: >50% tier drop

**Temporal** (Time-of-Day):
- Risky: 2-6 AM when normally 7-9 PM

## Loss-Chasing Thresholds

**Bet After Loss Ratio**:
- <0.40: Normal
- 0.60-0.75: High risk
- >0.75: Critical

**Bet Escalation Ratio**:
- <1.2: Normal
- 1.5-2.0: High risk
- >2.0: Critical (doubling down)
